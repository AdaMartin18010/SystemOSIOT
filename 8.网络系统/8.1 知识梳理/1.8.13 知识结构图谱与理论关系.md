## 哲学批判 Philosophical Critique

- 知识结构图谱有助于整体把握理论体系，但需警惕结构化带来的简化与理论张力的遮蔽。
- Knowledge maps help grasp the overall theoretical system, but beware of simplification and the obscuring of theoretical tensions.
