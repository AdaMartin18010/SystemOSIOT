## 2. 标准条款原文

### 2.1 WebRTC RFC 8833 安全架构

> WebRTC MUST use DTLS to protect all data traffic. All implementations MUST support DTLS 1.2 with the TLS_ECDHE_ECDSA_WITH_AES_128_GCM_SHA256 cipher suite.
> — RFC 8833, Section 4.2
> WebRTC MUST use ICE (RFC 5245/RFC 8445) for NAT traversal. The use of STUN and TURN servers introduces trust considerations.
> — RFC 8833, Section 5

关键要求：

- 媒体与数据通道均通过 DTLS 加密；SRTP 密钥从 DTLS 导出。
- 身份验证：可使用 IdP（Identity Provider）机制或 DTLS 指纹校验。
- 隐私：ICE candidate 可能泄露本地 IP 地址；需通过 mDNS 或 TURN-only 模式降低泄露。

### 2.2 W3C DID Core

> A DID is a URI that associates a DID subject with a DID document so that the subject can be cryptographically verified.
> — W3C DID Core, Section 3.1

关键概念：

- DID Subject：被标识的实体。
- DID Document：包含 verification method（公钥）、authentication、service endpoint。
- DID Method：定义如何在特定分布式账本上创建、读取、更新、撤销 DID。

### 2.3 W3C Verifiable Credentials Data Model 2.0

> A verifiable credential is a tamper-evident credential that has authorship that can be cryptographically verified.
> — W3C VC Data Model 2.0, Section 1.2

关键概念：

- Issuer、Holder、Verifier 三方模型。
- Verifiable Presentation：Holder 向 Verifier 出示的一个或多个 VC，可附加零知识证明。

### 2.4 GDPR 相关条款

> Personal data shall be processed lawfully, fairly and in a transparent manner (Article 5).
> — GDPR Art. 5(1)(a)
> The controller shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure and to be able to demonstrate that processing is performed in accordance with this Regulation (Article 25).
> Taking into account the state of the art, the costs of implementation and the nature, scope, context and purposes of processing … the controller and the processor shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk (Article 32).

### 2.5 EU eIDAS Regulation

> Electronic signatures, electronic seals, electronic time stamps, electronic registered delivery services and electronic identification schemes may be granted qualified status under this Regulation.

关键影响：

- 在欧盟范围内，qualified electronic signature 具有与手写签名同等的法律效力。
- 去中心化身份系统若面向欧盟用户，需评估与 eIDAS 信任框架的兼容性。
